Hi! My name is Elijah Frankle. I'm an undergraduate at the University of California Santa Barbara's College of Creative Studies. My major is Computer Science and I have a double minor in Theater Design and Media Arts.

With a passion for interdisciplinary work and collaboration, I seek to explore the intersection of art, technology, and innovation to create new experiences. I'm fueled by an endless curiosity which is always pushing me forward into the unknown. I thrive in fast-paced collaborative environments where I can work with others to find clever solutions to unusual problems. I can't wait to see where I go next.
